## Aesthetic

Orbit Clarity combines clean geometric surfaces with calm, spacious interactions inspired by Todoist and Linear. Cards use subtle shadows and generous padding on cool gray backgrounds. Interactive elements have smooth 150-200ms transitions. The indigo brand color appears only in primary actions and active states — hover states use neutral tones to maintain focus hierarchy.
